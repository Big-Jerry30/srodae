Our Ministries

Serving God and our community through diverse areas of ministry

Empowering Lives Through Service

Our nine ministries work together to build a strong, vibrant community of believers

👶

Children's Ministry

Nurturing the next generation with biblical foundations and age-appropriate teachings. Our children's ministry creates a fun, safe environment where children learn about God's love.

  • Sunday School classes
  • Bible stories and lessons
  • Character development programs
  • Children's choir and activities
🎯

Youth Ministry

Empowering young people to live purposeful lives anchored in Christ. We provide mentorship, discipleship, and opportunities for growth and service.

  • Youth fellowship meetings
  • Leadership training
  • Talent development
  • Evangelism and outreach
📚

PENSA

Pentecost Students and Associates - Supporting students in their academic and spiritual journey. We bridge faith and education for student success.

  • Campus ministry
  • Academic support groups
  • Prayer and Bible study
  • Student conferences
👩

Women's Ministry

Building strong women of faith who impact their families and communities. We provide support, training, and fellowship for women of all ages.

  • Women's fellowship
  • Marriage and family seminars
  • Skills training programs
  • Prayer groups
👨

Men's Ministry

Raising godly men who lead with integrity and strength. Men's ministry challenges and equips men to be spiritual leaders in their homes and communities.

  • Men's fellowship
  • Brotherhood and accountability
  • Leadership development
  • Community service projects
📢

Evangelism Ministry

Spreading the Gospel and winning souls for the Kingdom of God. We actively reach out to communities with the message of salvation and hope in Jesus Christ.

  • Street evangelism
  • House-to-house outreach
  • Crusades and campaigns
  • New convert follow-up
🤝

MPWD

Men and Women's Directorate - Coordinating and supporting ministry activities across gender groups. MPWD provides structure and vision for effective ministry.

  • Ministry coordination
  • Training and resources
  • Annual conferences
  • Strategic planning
🌍

Home & Urban Missions

Reaching communities for Christ through strategic missions work. We plant churches, support missionaries, and transform communities through the Gospel.

  • Church planting
  • Community development
  • Missionary support
  • Cross-cultural ministry
⛪

Chaplaincy Ministry

Providing spiritual care in institutions including schools, hospitals, prisons, and workplaces. Our chaplains offer pastoral support and ministry in specialized settings.

  • Hospital chaplaincy
  • School chaplaincy
  • Prison ministry
  • Corporate chaplaincy

Get Involved

Discover your calling and join a ministry that matches your passion and gifts. There's a place for you to serve!
