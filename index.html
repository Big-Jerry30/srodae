A Place to Call Home

The Church of Pentecost, New Juaben District, is a vibrant community of believers dedicated to spreading the Gospel and transforming lives. With seven local assemblies across the region, we serve as a beacon of hope and faith.

Whether you're seeking spiritual growth, community connection, or a place to serve, you'll find a warm welcome here. Our district is committed to excellence in worship, discipleship, and outreach.
